# Introduction

Welcome to **AI Workflows**! This site is a curated resource for developers setting up local AI development workflows, sharing learnings, and documenting best practices.

Here you will find step-by-step guides, shell configurations, and tool recommendations to make your AI-assisted coding experience fast, seamless, and powerful.

---

## Key Sections

- **[Getting Started](/docs/getting-started)**: Setup your macOS or Linux machine with Homebrew, Python, `uv`, and `headroom-ai`.
- **[AI Curriculum](/docs/category/ai-curriculum)**: Master the principles of agent steering and context engineering.
  - **[AI Workflows & Shape & Ship](/docs/curriculum/concepts)**: The core methodology of collaborating with coding agents.
  - **[Using AI Skills](/docs/curriculum/using-skills)**: Understanding how agents consume context-specific instruction files.
  - **[Creating AI Skills](/docs/curriculum/creating-skills)**: A guide to authoring your own custom `SKILL.md` documents.
  - **[Secure Remote Agents](/docs/curriculum/hermes-tailscale)**: Setting up Tailscale, Hermes WebUI, and Claude Code for safe remote access.
- **Workflows & Guides**: Detailed walkthroughs of custom agent configurations and integrations. (Coming soon!)

---

## Target Platforms

For now, our setup guides and workflows focus on:
- **macOS**
- **Linux**

We plan to expand support to **Windows** in the near future.
